More than a website

Your work needs a place.

A Digital Home is the centre of an artist’s public life: it does not replace social media, it organises it. It is where programmers, journalists, students, audiences and partners quickly understand who you are, what you do, where to listen, how to contact you and how to share your work.

IdentityBiography, manifesto, photography, videos and visual language.
DiscoverySEO, keywords, bilingual structure and pages designed to be found.
ConversionContact, newsletter, forms, calendar and clear calls to action.

What it should include

A digital presence that breathes.

Artistic portrait

Short and long biography, signature phrase, official photos, repertoire, projects and artistic path.

Agenda and news

Concerts, masterclasses, releases, latest news and links to Instagram, Facebook, YouTube or Spotify.

Media kit

Press photos, approved copy, videos, professional contact details, downloads and technical information.

Immersive experience

Clean design, subtle motion, audio/video, storytelling and mobile-first navigation.

SEO for artists

Structure prepared for searches around artist, conductor, choir, ensemble, city, country, repertoire and services.

Simple maintenance

A system to update agenda, images, texts and links without losing visual coherence.
