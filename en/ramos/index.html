Ramos brings international conductors, individual singers and choirs to Portugal for intensive rehearsals, masterclasses and sacred-music concerts.

Every year during Palm Sunday week, different ways of listening, feeling and building music transform singers and audiences in Cascais and Lisbon.

Participation

Choose your path.

International individual singer
  1. Review the edition repertoire.
  2. Choose two works and record complete singing videos.
  3. Email the links and a short musical biography to info@voxlaci.com.
  4. Wait for the guest conductor's decision.

After acceptance and payment of the €295 Ramos fee, singers receive PDF scores and preparation audio. Travel and accommodation are not included.

Singer resident in Portugal
The application process is the same. Accepted singers may join the pre-festival preparation rehearsals.
Participating choir
Choirs can propose collective participation. Conditions depend on the number of singers, repertoire, rehearsals and local logistics.
Conducting masterclass
Send a short musical biography. The programme explores vulnerability, curiosity, communication, repertoire, silent conducting, positive feedback and individual mentoring.
Location and payment
